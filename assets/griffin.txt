Anesthesia Considerations:
- Induction: Combination of ketamine (5 mg/kg) and midazolam (0.2 mg/kg) IM.
- Maintenance: Isoflurane or sevoflurane with oxygen.
- Muscle relaxation: Consider gallamine triethiodide for its effects on cardiac vagus.
- Monitoring: Separate monitoring for avian and mammalian parts. Use multi-species compatible equipment.
- Recovery: Provide a large, padded area to prevent injury during recovery.
- Special considerations: Beak and talon safety precautions, feather-friendly positioning and draping.
