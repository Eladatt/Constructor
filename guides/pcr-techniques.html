Key Concepts

Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a method to amplify specific DNA sequences. It involves denaturation, annealing, and extension steps.

PCR Protocol

1. Prepare PCR mix
2. Initial denaturation: 95°C, 5 min
3. 30-35 cycles of:
   a. Denaturation: 95°C, 30 sec
   b. Annealing: 50-65°C, 30 sec
   c. Extension: 72°C, 1 min/kb
4. Final extension: 72°C, 5-10 min
5. Hold at 4°C
