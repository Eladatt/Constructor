1. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • Always wear safety goggles
  • Use appropriate gloves for chemical handling
  • Wear closed-toe shoes and long pants

2. Chemical Handling

  • Read Safety Data Sheets (SDS) before using any chemical
  • Use fume hoods when working with volatile substances
  • Never pipette by mouth

3. Emergency Procedures

  • Know the location of fire extinguishers, eyewash stations, and safety showers
  • Familiarize yourself with evacuation routes
  • Report all accidents and injuries immediately
